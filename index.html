Coffee, kitchen, and quiet moments — thoughtfully crafted.

Amani was created with a simple intention: to share food that brings comfort, connection, and a sense of peace.

Our story is shaped by late night chai conversations, family kitchens filled with the scent of cardamom and butter, and recipes passed down, reimagined, and shared with love. What started as a small dessert concept centered around our signature flavored milk cakes and savory, flaky bites has grown into something more. It is now where cultures meet, where tradition feels modern, and where every detail is intentional.

Each milk cake is crafted to be soft, indulgent, and deeply nostalgic. Each savory bite is layered, buttery, and made to be shared. Every cup of coffee is an invitation to slow down.

Amani means peace, and that is what we hope you feel in every bite and every sip.

Amani is a feeling. A quiet moment in a busy day. A reminder of home, wherever you come from.

Amani welcomes you to share what we love most: food that feels like care and experiences that leave you just a little more at peace than when you arrived.
